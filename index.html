About Us

PharmaShield is a blockchain-based platform to track the lifecycle of prescription drugs from manufacturing to distribution to retail ensuring transparency and authenticity.

Protecting Lives Through Authentic Medication

PharmaShield believes everyone deserves access to safe, genuine medicine. Using blockchain and QR code technology, we track each drug's journey from manufacturer to consumer, helping eliminate counterfeit medications and protect patient health.

Empowering Trust with Every Scan

With just one scan, users, whether patients or providers, can instantly verify a medication's authenticity. PharmaShield promotes transparency, trust, and safety across the entire pharmaceutical supply chain.
